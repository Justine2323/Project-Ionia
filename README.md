## Part 1: Planning
<p align="justify">
Project-Ionia focuses on integrating the mechanical design, electronics, and control strategy of a 20Ã—20 sumobot for the ASICUP competition into one unified system before construction. This stage defines how the Arduino Uno, 490 RPM worm gear DC motors, two Sharp IR sensors, and three ultrasonic sensors will work together through proper sensor placement, motor configuration, and wiring layout to achieve reliable opponent detection, strong pushing capability, and accurate edge avoidance.
